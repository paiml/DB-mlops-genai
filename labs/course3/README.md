# Course 3: MLOps Engineering Labs

## Lab Structure

Each lab includes:
- Databricks notebook (`.py` export)
- Sovereign AI Stack equivalent (Rust)
- Hands-on exercises

## Labs by Week

### Week 1: Experiment Tracking & Feature Engineering
- `lab_mlflow.py`: Build MLflow Client

### Week 2: Model Training & Serving
- `lab_training.py`: Train Models with aprender
- `lab_serving.py`: Serve Models with realizar

### Week 3: Production & Capstone
- `lab_quality.py`: Enforce Quality Gates
